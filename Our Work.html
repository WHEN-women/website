Point Blank Period aims to alleviate menstrual inequity by:

Educate: Increasing public awareness about persistent local and global disparities.
Advocate: Equipping allies with the tools to facilitate broader sociopolitical change.
Donate: Providing marginalized menstruators with access to free, diverse, healthy, and sustainable options.
Celebrate: Destigmatizing menstruation, such that individuals can manage their periods healthily and without shame.
PointBlankPeriod 2021
In accordance with World Menstrual Hygiene Day, Point Blank Period launched a month-long educational campaign in support of menstrual equity. In collaboration with various non-profit organizations, the series aims to inform readers about prevailing menstrual disparities and provide allies with the resources to effect tangible change.
Our Impact
WHEN also donated comprehensive menstrual packages to street-involved youth accessing Yonge Street Mission services in the Greater Toronto Area. In accordance with numerous local, national, and international businesses, we provided recipients with an assortment of personal and menstrual care items, such that they can manage their periods comfortably, healthily and without shame.
